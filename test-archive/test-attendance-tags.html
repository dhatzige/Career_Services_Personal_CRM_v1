Test Attendance Tags

Instructions:

  1. Open the application at http://localhost:5173
  2. Login with your credentials
  3. Navigate to the Students page
  4. Look for students who have consultations in the past week
  5. You should see attendance tags next to their status and year showing:
    • Green tag with checkmark: Attended consultations
    • Red tag with X: No-show consultations
    • Yellow tag with alert: Cancelled consultations
    • Gray tag with clock: Upcoming or scheduled consultations
  6. The tags should show the date (e.g., "Jan 30 - Attended")

To Add Test Data

If you don't see any consultations, you can add some test data:

  1. Click on a student to open their details
  2. Add a new consultation with a date within the past 7 days
  3. Set the attendance status
  4. Save and go back to the students list
  5. The attendance tag should now appear on the student card
